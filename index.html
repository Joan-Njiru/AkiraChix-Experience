My AkiraChix Experience

My experience at akiraChix so far has been a series of highs and lows.
So much has happened in the one week i've been here that i've gotten to
understand how things work. Most of what i've experienced so far I really
like while the rest has made me take a moment to rethink my habits.

Some of the things i've loved so far are;

  1. The food has been really good.
  2. I've made some really great friends.
  3. The study resouces we have here are top tier.
  4. The place is really beautiful.
  5. I've enjoyed all my classes so far.

Some of the things that i've had to adjust my habits for include;

  • Duties have to be done on time to avoid punishment.
  • Not submitting assignments on time leads to punishment.
  • There is a dress code to follow.
